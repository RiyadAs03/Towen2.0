About Us

TechKnightz is not just a student association; it's a community of passionate tech enthusiasts coming together to create a brighter future through technology. Established in 2022 by 2020-2024 Batch , TechKnightz is a proud initiative of Department of Computer Science and Business Systems[CSBS], driven by a shared vision of fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration among students and below students who make TechKnightz.
